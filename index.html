My Journey as a Student

Life of a grade school student is different from the life of a college student. As a first year college student, it was so hard to adjust and catch up with what was going on. Life humbled me, to the point I looked down upon myself. I thought maybe college was not for me, but instead of giving up I chose to continue and pursue.

I can define myself as a dedicated person, someone who is willing and eager to acquire new knowledge. I am compassionate in achieving my goals in life and motivated to reach my aspirations.

Honestly, I still don’t fully know who I am, but I do know I am a student with purpose, worth, and value. I chose Information Technology because I have loved computers since I was 9 years old. That’s why I took this course. I told myself, <“What if I try?> There’s no wrong in trying. If somebody can do this, I can do this too.”

I want to enhance my analytical skills, learn coding and programming, and grow through every struggle. Despite the hardest battles, I strive to remain genuine and wise. I believe that many opportunities are waiting, whatever path I choose.

Isaiah 30:18

“Be patient, sometimes God makes us wait longer because the blessing is bigger.”
